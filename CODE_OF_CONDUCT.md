# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as maintainers and contributors pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported by opening a confidential issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and any behavior deemed inappropriate will be addressed.
